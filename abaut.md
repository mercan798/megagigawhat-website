# About — Mega Giga What?

## Welcome to Your Data Calculation Hub

**Mega Giga What?** is a free online platform designed to help professionals, businesses, and individuals understand and calculate data transfer times and cloud storage costs. In today's data-driven world, understanding these metrics is crucial for making informed decisions about your infrastructure and budget.

## Our Mission

We believe that everyone should have access to simple, accurate tools for calculating data-related metrics. Whether you're a system administrator planning a data migration, a business owner budgeting for cloud storage, or a student learning about data transfer rates, our calculators provide instant, reliable results.

## What We Offer

### Transfer Time Calculator
Calculate exactly how long it will take to transfer large amounts of data over your network connection. Simply input:
- **Data Amount**: Specify your data size in terabytes (TB)
- **Connection Speed**: Enter your network speed in megabits per second (Mbit/s)

Get instant results showing transfer time broken down into days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

### Storage Cost Calculator
Plan your cloud storage budget with precision. Our calculator helps you estimate:
- Monthly storage costs based on your data volume
- Long-term expenses for multi-month or multi-year planning
- Cost projections for AWS RDS and other cloud platforms
